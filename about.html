Our Story

Established in 2019, Chitwan IT Solutions Pvt. Ltd. has rapidly grown to become one of Nepal's most trusted names in CCTV surveillance solutions and telecommunication services. With a strong foundation built on quality, integrity, and customer satisfaction, we have successfully sold and installed over 50,000 CCTV units across Nepal.

Our services are not just product-oriented but solution-focused—tailored to ensure security, connectivity, and long-term value for our clients.
