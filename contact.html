Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions about our services and support

How quickly can you respond to service requests?

+

We typically respond to service requests within 2-4 hours during business hours. For emergency situations, we prioritize immediate response to minimize downtime for your business.

Do you offer maintenance contracts for your solutions?

+

Yes, we offer flexible maintenance contracts for all our solutions including networking, CCTV, and IT support services. These contracts include regular check-ups, updates, and priority support.

What areas do you serve in Nepal?

+

While our main office is in Chitwan, we serve clients throughout Nepal including Kathmandu, Pokhara, Butwal, and other major cities. We can arrange on-site visits as needed.

Do you provide warranty for your products and services?

+

Yes, we provide warranty on all our products and services. The warranty period varies depending on the product and service, which we clearly outline in our service agreements.

Can you help with existing IT infrastructure?

+

Absolutely! We can assess, maintain, upgrade, or integrate with your existing IT infrastructure. Our team is experienced with various systems and technologies.
